Starting a community

While as a Co-Founder of a meetup in London, being able to meet so many designers and other people involved in these events, I realized about the power of a community.

The meetings helped so many people getting their first steps into UX Design. Not only by creating insights into the design thinking process but as well through portfolio feedback, overcome impostor syndrome, manage discussions into specific problems aiming to find solutions and support and encouragement through the job hunting process and while being a UX Designer.

Volunteering with Tech Circus (London)

I love Tech Circus meetups - UX Crunch (London, UK).

The team behind the scenes are very open and genuinely care about all the community.

In 2019, I directly contacted Tech Circus to get involved in the UX Live 2019 event and they received me with open arms, as well my closer community. Was such a wonderful surprise!

This was the start of an exciting experience of collaboration, close connections and network expansion while learning new skills and growing my career through educational content and in direct contact with more experienced designers.
