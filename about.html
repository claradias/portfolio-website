Work About Resume

Hi! I'm Clara!

I'm a UX/UI Designer from Portugal currently based in London, U.K.

I'm passionate about people and working with people.

I ended up becoming a UX/UI designer after experiencing working in other sectors, such as healthcare, arts and education. These experiences brought me a holistic and empathic approach to solving problems and understanding the needs and motivations of others.

My ambition as a designer is timeless aesthetics that meets functionality and ultimately have a valuable and positive impact on people's lives.

I am a strong believer in a more fair and inclusive world and thrive to self-growth.

In my spare time, I make art, travel, cycle and practise yoga; never say no to a good gig and good coffee. I'm an unapologetic cat person.
