Hi, I'm Clara.

After years of studying, travelling, changing and challenging myself in different careers, I was introduced to UX Design. Through my diverse background experience in nursing, fine arts and education, I developed a passion for a collaborative and empathic approach to problem-solving.

My goal is to turn ideas into useful, intuitive, ethical and engaging products to millions for people with a clear and user-focused design aesthetic. I am determined to have a positive impact on the way people use products and I am a strong believer on a more fair and inclusive world.

In my spare time, I build communities and help people to pursue their passions.

Aside from this, I make art, I travel a bit (20 countries and counting), cycle around and practice yoga. I never say no to a good gig, good coffee and I try my best to keep a sustainable living. I'm an unapologetic cat person.
