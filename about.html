About

Hi! I'm Clara and I'm a UX/UI Designer from Portugal currently based in London, UK.

I'm passionate about people and working with people.

But I have not always been a designer...

I was introduced to UX Design after experiencing working in other sectors such as nursing, fine arts and education. These experiences have enabled my understanding of the needs and motivations of others, as well as collaborative and empathic approach to problem-solving that are key elements to design human-centered experiences.

My goal as a designer is to turn ideas into useful, meaningful and engaging products to millions for people with a clear and user-focused design aesthetic. I am determined to have a positive impact on the way people use products and I am a strong believer on a more fair and inclusive world.

In my spare time, I make art, travel, cycle and practice yoga. I never say no to a good gig and good coffee. I'm an unapologetic cat person.
