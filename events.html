Volunteering with Tech Circus (London)

I love Tech Circus meetups - Junior UX Crunch and UX Crunch in London. The team behind the scenes are very open and genuinely care about all the community.

In 2019, I directly contacted Tech Circus to get involved in UX Live 2019 event and they received me with open arms, extending them as well to everyone interested in my closer community. Was such a wonderful surprise, I had to grab this opportunity!

This was the start of an exciting experience of collaboration, close connections and network expansion while learning new skills and growing my career through educational content and in direct contact with more experienced designers.

Since then ive been volunteering at their events
